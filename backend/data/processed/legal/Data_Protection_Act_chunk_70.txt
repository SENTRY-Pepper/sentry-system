41. Data protection by design or by default
(1) Every data controller or data processor shall implement appropriate technical and organisational
measures which are designed—
(a) to implement the data protection principles in an effective manner; and
(b) to integrate necessary safeguards for that purpose into the processing.
(2) The duty under subsection (1) applies both at the time of the determination of the means of
processing the data and at the time of the processing.
