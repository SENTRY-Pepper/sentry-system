Part XI – MISCELLANEOUS PROVISIONS
72. Offences of unlawful disclosure of personal data
(1) A data controller who, without lawful excuse, discloses personal data in any manner that is
incompatible with the purpose for which such data has been collected commits an offence.
(2) A data processor who, without lawful excuse, discloses personal data processed by the data
processor without the prior authority of the data controller commits an offence.
