69. Accounts and Audit
The annual accounts of the Office shall be prepared, audited and reported in accordance with the
provisions of Articles 226 and 229 of the Constitution, the Public Finance Management Act (Cap. 412A), or
any other law relating to audit of public entities.
70. Annual reports
(1) The Data Commissioner shall, within three months after the end of each financial year, prepare
and submit to the Cabinet Secretary a report of the operations of the Office for the immediately
preceding year.
(2) The Cabinet Secretary shall submit the annual report before the National Assembly within three
months of receipt of the report under subsection (1).
