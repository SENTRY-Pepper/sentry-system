58. Enforcement notices
(1) Where the Data Commissioner is satisfied that a person has failed, or is failing, to comply with
any provision of this Act, the Data Commissioner may serve an enforcement notice on that person
requiring that person to take such steps and within such period as may be specified in the notice.
