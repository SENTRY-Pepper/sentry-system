59. Power to seek assistance
For the purpose of gathering information or for any investigation under this Act, the Data Commissioner
may seek the assistance of such person or authority as they deem fit and as is reasonably necessary to
assist the Data Commissioner in the discharge of their functions.
60. Power of entry and search
The Data Commissioner, upon obtaining a warrant from a Court, may enter and search any premises for
the purpose of discharging any function or exercising any power under this Act.
