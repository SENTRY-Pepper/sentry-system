31. Data protection impact assessment
(1) Where a processing operation is likely to result in high risk to the rights and freedoms of a data
subject, by virtue of its nature, scope, context and purposes, a data controller or data processor
shall, prior to the processing, carry out a data protection impact assessment.
(2) A data protection impact assessment shall include the following—
(a) a systematic description of the envisaged processing operations and the purposes of
the processing, including, where applicable, the legitimate interest pursued by the data
controller or data processor;
(b) an assessment of the necessity and proportionality of the processing operations in relation
to the purposes;
