74. Codes, guidelines and certification
(1) The Data Commissioner may, for the purpose of this Act—
(a) issue guidelines or codes of practice for the data controllers, data processors and data
protection officers;

encourage compliance of processing operations with this Act;
(c) require certification or adherence to code of practice by a third party;
