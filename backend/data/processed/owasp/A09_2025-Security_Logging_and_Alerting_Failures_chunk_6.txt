Auditable events, such as logins, failed logins, and highvalue transactions, are not logged or logged inconsistently (for instance, only logging successful logins, but not failed attempts).
 Warnings and errors generate no, inadequate, or unclear log messages.
 The integrity of logs is not properly protected from tampering.
 Logs of applications and APIs are not monitored for suspicious activity.
 Logs are only stored locally, and not properly backedup.
