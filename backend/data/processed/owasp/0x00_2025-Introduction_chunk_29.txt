Similar to what we did for the 2021 edition, we leveraged CVE data for Exploitability and (Technical) Impact. We downloaded OWASP Dependency Check and extracted the CVSS Exploit and Impact scores, grouping them by relevant CWEs listed with the CVEs. It took a fair bit of research and effort, as all the CVEs have CVSSv2 scores, but there are flaws in CVSSv2 that CVSSv3 should address.
