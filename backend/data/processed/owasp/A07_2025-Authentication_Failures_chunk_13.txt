Scenario 1: Credential stuffing, the use of lists of known username and password combinations, is now a very common attack. More recently attackers have been found to ‘increment’ or otherwise adjust passwords, based on common human behavior. For instance, changing ‘Winter2025’ to ‘Winter2026’, or ‘ILoveMyDog6’ to ‘ILoveMyDog7’ or ‘ILoveMyDog5’.
