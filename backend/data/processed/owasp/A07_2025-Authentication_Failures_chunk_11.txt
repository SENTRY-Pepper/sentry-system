Use a serverside, secure, builtin session manager that generates a new random session ID with high entropy after login. Session identifiers should not be in the URL, be securely stored in a secure cookie, and invalidated after logout, idle, and absolute timeouts. 

 Ideally, use a premade, welltrusted system to handle authentication, identity, and session management. Transfer this risk whenever possible by buying and utilizing a hardened and well tested system.
