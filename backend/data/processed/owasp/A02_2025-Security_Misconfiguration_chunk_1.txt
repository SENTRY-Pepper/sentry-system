Background. 

Moving up from 5 in the previous edition, 100% of the applications tested were found to have some form of misconfiguration, with an average incidence rate of 3.00%, and over 719k occurrences of a Common Weakness Enumeration (CWE) in this risk category. With more shifts into highly configurable software, it's not surprising to see this category moving up. Notable CWEs included are CWE16 Configuration and CWE611 Improper Restriction of XML External Entity Reference (XXE).
