One of the difficulties of using the OWASP Top 10 as a standard is that we document AppSec risks, and not necessarily easily testable issues. For example, A06:2025Insecure Design is beyond the scope of most forms of testing. Another example is testing whether inplace, inuse, and effective logging and monitoring are implemented, which can only be done with interviews and requesting a sampling of effective incident responses.
