If you already have an application security program, consider performing a maturity assessment on it using OWASP SAMM (Software Assurance Maturity Model) or DSOMM (DevSecOps Maturity Model) . These maturity models are comprehensive and exhaustive and can be used to help you figure out where you should best focus your efforts for expanding and maturing your program.
